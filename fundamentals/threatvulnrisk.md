# ⚠️ Threat vs Vulnerability vs Risk

## 📌 What are These Terms?

These are core cybersecurity concepts:

- **Threat** → Potential danger  
- **Vulnerability** → Weakness  
- **Risk** → Impact of threat exploiting vulnerability  

Threat, vulnerability और risk cybersecurity के सबसे important concepts हैं।

---

## 🧠 Understanding Through a Scenario

Momo uses a weak password for his account.

- Threat → Hacker  
- Vulnerability → Weak password  
- Risk → Account gets hacked  

👉 Together they create a security problem.

---

## 🔑 Key Concept

👉 Risk exists only when:

- Threat + Vulnerability + Impact

If no vulnerability → no risk  
अगर weakness नहीं है, तो risk भी नहीं होगा।

---

## 🔄 Simple Analogy

- Threat → Thief  
- Vulnerability → Open door  
- Risk → Theft  

---

## 📊 Business Perspective

- Helps in risk management  
- Used in decision making  
- Prioritizes security efforts  

---

## ⚠️ Common Issues

- Confusing terms  
- Ignoring vulnerabilities  
- Not assessing impact  

---

## 🛡️ Real-World Attacks

- Weak passwords → account takeover  
- Unpatched systems → malware attack

## 🎯 Interview Tips

## 🚀 Key Takeaways
