# 🛡️ CIA Triad in Cybersecurity

## 📌 What is CIA Triad?

The CIA Triad is a simple model that explains what cybersecurity is trying to protect, keeping data private, accurate, and available when needed.

CIA Triad का मतलब है, data को सुरक्षित रखना, सही रखना और ज़रूरत पड़ने पर उपलब्ध रखना।

---

## 🔐 Confidentiality

Confidentiality means making sure that only the right people can see the data.

Confidentiality का मतलब है कि data सिर्फ उन्हीं लोगों को दिखे जिन्हें इसकी अनुमति है।

For example, when Rohit checks his bank balance on his phone, he expects that only he can view that information. If someone else, whether a person nearby or a hacker, is able to see it without permission, confidentiality is broken.

जैसे आपका ATM PIN या OTP सिर्फ आपको ही पता होना चाहिए, अगर कोई और जान जाए, तो आपकी privacy खत्म हो जाती है।

---

## 🧾 Integrity

Integrity means ensuring that data remains correct and is not changed without authorization.

Integrity का मतलब है कि data सही रहे और बिना अनुमति के बदला न जाए।

Imagine Priya sends ₹5000 to her friend using a payment app. She expects the system to record the exact amount. If due to an issue or attack the amount changes to ₹500, the data has been altered, and integrity is lost.

जैसे आपने ₹5000 भेजे, लेकिन सिस्टम में ₹500 दिखे, इसका मतलब data सही नहीं रहा।

---

## ⚡ Availability

Availability means systems and data should be accessible whenever users need them.

Availability का मतलब है कि system और data ज़रूरत पड़ने पर उपलब्ध रहें।

Aman waits until the last day to fill his exam form. When he finally tries to open the website, it crashes due to heavy traffic. Even though the system exists, he cannot use it at that moment, this is an availability issue.

जैसे exam form भरने के दिन वेबसाइट crash हो जाए, यह availability problem है।

---

## 🎯 Interview Tip

Explain concepts using real-life situations, it shows practical understanding.

---

## 🚀 Key Takeaways

- CIA Triad is the foundation of cybersecurity  
- It focuses on privacy, accuracy, and accessibility  
- Most real-world issues map to one of these three
