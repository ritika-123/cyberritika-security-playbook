# 🔐 Digital Signatures in Cybersecurity

## 📌 What is a Digital Signature?

Digital Signature is used to verify:

- Identity of sender  
- Integrity of data  

Digital Signature का मतलब है यह verify करना कि data किसने भेजा है और उसमें कोई बदलाव नहीं हुआ।

---

## 🔐 How Digital Signatures Work

Momo sends an important document.

- He signs it using his private key  
- Receiver verifies it using Momo’s public key  

If data is changed, the signature becomes invalid.

---

## ⚠️ When Digital Signatures Fail

If Momo’s private key is stolen:

- Attacker can sign fake documents  
- Identity can be misused  

---

## 🎯 Interview Tip

Digital signatures provide **authentication, integrity, and non-repudiation**.

---

## 🚀 Key Takeaways

- Ensures trust in communication  
- Uses asymmetric encryption  
- Protects data integrity
