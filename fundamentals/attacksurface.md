# 🎯 Attack Surface

## 📌 What is Attack Surface?

Attack surface is the **total number of entry points** where an attacker can try to exploit a system.

Attack surface का मतलब है system में जितने भी possible entry points हैं attacker के लिए।

---

## 🧠 Understanding Through a Scenario

Momo’s application has:

- Open ports  
- APIs  
- Login page  
- Admin panel  

👉 All these increase attack surface.

---

## 🔑 Key Concept

- More entry points = higher risk  
- Less exposure = better security  

---

## 🔄 Types of Attack Surface

### 1. Network Attack Surface
- Open ports  
- Services  

### 2. Application Attack Surface
- APIs  
- Forms  

### 3. Physical Attack Surface
- Devices  
- Hardware  

---

## 📊 Business Perspective

- Larger attack surface = higher risk  
- Harder to manage security  
- More chances of breach  

---

## ⚠️ Common Issues

- Unused open ports  
- Exposed endpoints  
- Misconfigured services  

---

## 🛡️ Real-World Example

- Open admin panel → unauthorized access  
- Public API → data leak  

---

## 🎯 Interview Tips

- Define clearly  
- Give real examples  
- Mention reduction strategies  

---

## 🚀 Key Takeaways

- Reduce unnecessary exposure  
- Smaller attack surface = safer system
