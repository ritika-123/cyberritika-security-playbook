# 🔐 Certificates & Certificate Authorities

## 📌 What are Certificates?

A certificate is a **digital identity proof** that verifies the authenticity of a system or user.

Certificate का मतलब है digital पहचान (identity proof)।

---

## 📌 What is a Certificate Authority (CA)?

A Certificate Authority (CA) is a **trusted organization** that issues certificates.

CA trusted entity होती है जो verify करके certificate देती है।

---

## 🔐 How It Works

Momo visits a secure platform.

- The platform shows a certificate  
- Momo’s system checks if it is issued by a trusted CA  
- If valid → connection is trusted  

Momo तभी trust करता है जब certificate valid हो।

---

## ⚠️ When It Fails

If a fake or compromised certificate is used:

- Momo may trust a malicious system  
- Data can be intercepted  

अगर CA compromise हो जाए, तो trust system टूट जाता है।

---

## 🎯 Interview Tip

Certificates = Identity  
CA = Trust provider  

---

## 🚀 Key Takeaways

- Certificates verify identity  
- CA builds trust  
- Essential for secure systems
